For God's Glory Ministry believes:


-The Lord is truthful: "As for God, his way is perfect: The Lord’s word is flawless; he shields all who take refuge in him." ~2 Samuel 22:31

-The Bible was divinely inspired by God, therefore it is true: "All Scripture is God-breathed and is useful for teaching, rebuking, correcting and training in righteousness," ~2 Timothy 3:16

-Jesus died on the cross to save us from our sins and He rose again to prepare a place for us in heaven: "But God demonstrates his own love for us in this: While we were still sinners, Christ died for us." ~Romans 5:8, "After his suffering, he presented himself to them and gave many convincing proofs that he was alive. He appeared to them over a period of forty days and spoke about the kingdom of God." ~Acts 1:3

-Salvation: "And Peter said to them, “Repent and be baptized every one of you in the name of Jesus Christ for the forgiveness of your sins, and you will receive the gift of the Holy Spirit." ~Acts 2:38 "If you declare with your mouth, “Jesus is Lord,” and believe in your heart that God raised him from the dead, you will be saved." ~Romans 10:9

-You must repent of your sin to live a life for God: "Repent, then, and turn to God, so that your sins may be wiped out, that times of refreshing may come from the Lord," ~Acts 3:19


Our Mission:

-To reach and teach everyone with Biblical truths: "Therefore go and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, and teaching them to obey everything I have commanded you. And surely I am with you always, to the very end of the age." ~Matthew 28:19-20

-To share the Word by giving gifts: "Through Jesus, therefore, let us continually offer to God a sacrifice of praise—the fruit of lips that openly profess his name. And do not forget to do good and to share with others, for with such sacrifices God is pleased." ~Hebrews 13:15-16

-To provide everyone with Christian resources

-To encourage Christians to evanglelize

-To help others in an effort to bring them to Christ: "And we urge you, brothers and sisters, warn those who are idle and disruptive, encourage the disheartened, help the weak, be patient with everyone." ~1 Thessalonians 5:14

For God's Glory Ministry works to help Christians and non-Christians find true joy and redemption in Jesus Christ.
